## Line Integral
### Line Integrals for Scalar Functions
$$
\int^{b}_{a}f(x(t), y(t))\sqrt{(x'(t))^2 + (y'(t))^2}dt
$$
### Line Integrals for Vector Fields
$$
\int^{b}_{a}P(x(t), y(t))x'(t)dt + Q(x(t), y(t))y'(t)dt
$$
**NOTE:** When you're dealing with *scalar functions* direction of the curve doesn't matter but with *vector fields* it matters.

**NOTE:** Line integrals for vector fields are **PATH INDEPENDENT**
